About Me

Chong Thao was born in Thailand in 1989. When he was 3 years old, he and his family moved to the United States. They settled in Marysville, California when they arrived and lived there for 26 years. In 2011, he moved to Sacramento, California. A couple of years later, in 2016, he married his wife. Now, he lives with his beautiful wife and their baby son.


Chong commutes to Roseville every weekday for work. There, he teaches adults with disabilities as an instructor for Aim Higher Inc. He has been employed at Aim Higher Inc for 6 years. Before he was instructor, he was working as a program assistance. Recently, about a year ago, he was promoted to instructor. Now, he continues to work for Aim Higher Inc and enjoyed teaching adults with disabilities.
